I am trying to build a system of open data exchange to build a basis for more cooperative applications. I am also working on machine learning techniques to make better use of data.

I am also working as a part-time consultant, if you have a project about distributed data management with Clojure or machine learning and data analysis, feel free to reach out to me! I have done descriptive statistics for analysis in R, deep learning and bayesian statistics in Python with tensorflow and theano and some experience with Matlab and Julia.
